Our Services

What Waipal offers

Two clear services to help you protect what matters – your ideas, works, documents and sensitive files.

Intellectual Property Protection

Helps you prove that a specific document, design, text or file existed in a certain form at a certain time. Waipal creates a digital fingerprint and a blockchain certificate that may serve as supporting evidence in a dispute over authorship or ownership.

Suitable for: texts, photographs, designs, logos, contracts, research

Learn more →

Document Encryption

Helps securely store and protect sensitive documents. Uploaded files are encrypted to reduce the risk of unauthorised access and keep your important information under your control.

Suitable for: contracts, company documents, sensitive personal files
